About Me

Hi! My name is Stephen Reilly, and I am an inspiring software developer. Before I got into software developing, I studied Theatrical Light Design at Ball State University. I love being a creative person whether it is with light design or in one of my other passions, writing. I love coming up with stories to tell through my design work. After a few years in the real world, I wanted a change of scenery, so I decided to attend ElevenFifty Academy's Software Development class. As the classes have gone on, the more I have been comfortable using my creative mind. It is my mission to use that feeling into any job you give me.
